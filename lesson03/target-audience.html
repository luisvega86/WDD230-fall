Target Audeince and Scenarios

Mar del Plata is a tourist destination par excellence located on the Atlantic coast of Argentina. The target audience of the Mardel Chamber of Commerce are those people who come to visit our city and even though those who actually live in Mar del Plata. We create a place where they can access from any place or device and find updated information about the activities that take place in the city, and the local businesses promote their products and services.
